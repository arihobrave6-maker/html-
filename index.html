♻️ Recycling Tips for Kampala Homes

  • Rinse plastic bottles before disposal
  • Start a home compost for kitchen scraps
  • Separate glass by color (clear, green, brown)
  • Never burn e-waste - take to collection points
  • Remove caps and labels from plastic containers
  • Flatten cardboard boxes to save space

📜 Key Laws & Regulations in Uganda

National Environment Act, 2019
Illegal dumping penalty: up to UGX 10 million or imprisonment
KCCA Waste Management Ordinance
All businesses must have waste collection contracts with licensed collectors
Plastic Ban, 2018
Thin plastic carrier bags (below 30 microns) are prohibited in Uganda
National Environment (Waste Management) Regulations, 2020
Sets standards for waste handling, storage, and disposal
